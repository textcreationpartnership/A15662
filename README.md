1. WITHER'S Motto.

    _ Nec habeo, nec Careo, nec Curo. Nor Haue I, nor Want I, nor Care I.

    _ Nec Habeo, nec Careo, nec Curo.

    _ Nec Habeo.

    _ Nec Careo.

    _ Nec Curo.

    _ To Enuy.

    _ A Postscript.
